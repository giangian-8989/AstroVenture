About

AstroVenture is a fun and interactive educational game designed for Grade 9 students to explore and learn about Space Science. Pilot your own rocket ship across a galaxy filled with stars, comets, planets, and galaxies. Along the way, test your knowledge with space-related questions and earn points to unlock exciting rocket ship skins. Mechanics

Launch Mission
1. Control Your Ship
- Use the W, A, S, D or the arrow keys to move the rocket ship freely across the space.
2. Collect Celestial Objects
-Fly through stars, rocks, planets, and galaxies to fill the progress bar on the top left side of the screen.
3. Answer Questions
-While collecting objects, space science questions will be triggered if the rocket is hit by incoming comets.
-Select the correct answer to fill the progress bar.
-Incorrect answers will cause you to lose one life.
-There will be a maximum of 10 questions per game.
-Once the progress bar is filled up and completed, the game is over.
4. Lives System
-You start with 3 lives (stars).
-Each wrong answer removes 1 life.
-The game ends when all lives are lost.
THIS IS A ONE TIME GAME.

QUIZ:
1. Select a Difficulty Level
-Easy: 10 questions
-Medium: 7 questions
-Hard: 5 questions
-Master: 5 timed questions (15 seconds each)
2. Answer the Questions
-Collect the correct answer from the scattered options in the space.
-Questions are about the solar system, gravity, and stars.
3. Master Mode:
-Each question has a 15-second timer.
-Wrong answers deduct 3 seconds from the timer.
-Unanswered questions skip automatically when time runs out.
4. Score
-Your score will be shown after all the questions are answered.
-A preview of the questions and the correct answers will be provided.
